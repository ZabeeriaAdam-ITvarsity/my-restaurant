Steak and Vegetables

Who doesn't love a beautifully cooked steak? Our delicious steak recipe is very simple and easy to prepare. Impress your family with this tasty treat, and don't forget to serve with golden baked vegetables.

Serves 4
Cook 15 mins
Prep 15 mins
36
Ingredients

Ingredients

Method

Method

  • Preheat oven to 200°C.
  • Whisk together the Knorr Creamy Cheese Vegetable Bake and boiling water, and set aside.
  • Combine the Stork Margarine, Robertsons Italian Herb and Robertsons Paprika in a bowl. Brush the steaks with the seasoning mixture. Place on a lined baking tray.
  • In a separate baking dish, add the onion, baby marrow and red pepper. Pour over creamy cheese Vegetable Bake mix.
  • Bake both the steaks and the vegetables for 10-15 minutes, or until vegetables are golden and steak is cooked as desired.
